Great American Ball Park

Cincinnati Reds

The Banks/Downtown CincinnatiCapacity: 42,319Opened: 2003

Overview

Great American Ball Park sits along the Ohio River with views of Cincinnati's skyline and the John A. Roebling Suspension Bridge. Known for the Reds Hall of Fame, Cincinnati chili, and the steamboat-inspired design elements.

Stadium Highlights

  • Ohio River and Cincinnati skyline views
  • Reds Hall of Fame and Museum
  • Steamboat-inspired architecture
  • The Gap entrance with statues

Unique Features

  • Power stacks resembling riverboat smokestacks
  • The Gap entrance plaza
  • Reds Hall of Fame and Museum
  • Machine Room Grille restaurant
  • Riverfront Stadium location
