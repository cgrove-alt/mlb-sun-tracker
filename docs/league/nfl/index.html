football Shade Tips

Best Game Times for Shade

  • • 13:00 - Day game (more sun exposure)
  • • 16:00 - Afternoon (moderate shade)
  • • 20:00 - Evening (better shade coverage)

Season Considerations

  • • Early season (September): Lower sun angle
  • • Mid-season: Peak sun exposure risk
  • • Late season (February): Better natural shade
  • • Cold weather games: Sun can be beneficial
