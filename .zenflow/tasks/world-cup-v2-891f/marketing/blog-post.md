## Why Shade Matters at World Cup 2026

### 1. **Summer Heat Across Three Countries**

The 2026 World Cup takes place during peak summer:
- **USA venues**: Temperatures ranging from 85-105¬∞F (29-40¬∞C)
- **Mexico venues**: High altitude + intense sun = extreme UV exposure
- **Canada venues**: Milder but still 70-80¬∞F (21-27¬∞C)

### 2. **Match Times and Sun Exposure**

Most matches will be scheduled during afternoon and early evening slots (2:00 PM - 6:00 PM local time) to accommodate global broadcast audiences. This means peak sun exposure for many games.

### 3. **Health and Safety**

Direct sun exposure for 2+ hours can lead to:
- Dehydration and heat exhaustion
- Sunburn and UV damage
- Reduced focus and enjoyment
- Risk of heat stroke in extreme conditions

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: When do World Cup 2026 tickets go on sale?
**A:** FIFA typically releases tickets in phases starting 12-18 months before the tournament. Follow official FIFA channels for announcements expected in late 2024/early 2025.

### Q: Can I change my seat after buying tickets?
**A:** Official FIFA tickets generally cannot be changed, but resale platforms may offer flexibility. Choose your seats carefully!

### Q: Do retractable roof stadiums guarantee shade?
**A:** Not always. Roof decisions depend on weather and FIFA preferences for "authentic soccer atmosphere." Check our venue pages for historical patterns.

### Q: Which venue has the best shade overall?
**A:** Indoor/covered venues (SoFi, AT&T, NRG, Mercedes-Benz, BC Place) offer best protection. Among open-air venues, Lumen Field and Gillette Stadium have the most favorable climates.

### Q: Is shade more important than sight lines?
**A:** Balance both! Use TheShadium to find shaded sections with good views. Generally, upper-level corners offer best shade while maintaining decent sight lines.

### Q: What if my match time changes?
**A:** FIFA may adjust kickoff times closer to the tournament. TheShadium's real-time calculator will update shade predictions for any time change.
